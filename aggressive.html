Born Without Aggression.

The following information is about how aggressive people think dogs are:

  • 34.8% believe that the breed of pitbulls are naturally more dangerous than other breeds.
  • 52.9% believe pitbulls are just as dangerous as any other breed
  • 12.3% believe pitbulls are naturally less dangerous
  • 93% of pit bulls in shelters are euthanized because of the harmful stereotype they are an aggressive breed.

Dogs are not born aggressive, it is what they were taught that makes them behave a certain way. It could be how their previous owners (or current owners) taught them to be. But with proper training, you can reteach your pup what is right from wrong.


Give a dog a chance, and don't take them to a shelter for one bad behavior. There is a reason, just like Ace in the movie League of Super Pets. End this stereotype of dogs are aggressive, when in reality they are adorable and go get a dog today.
