Proper Care

If this is your first time being a dog owner, don't worry we got you covered:

Everyday your pet will need
  • Plenty of Water
  • Food
  • Sleep
  • Daily walks (x3) to do their bathroom needs
  • Play time to stimulate their mind and learn
Hygiene needs
  • Their fur/coat needs to be brushed
  • Brush their teeth.
  • Monthly baths
  • Grooming
  • Nail Trimming
Medical needs
  • Yearly rabies shot
  • Yearly checkups
    • Ensure they are up-to-date with other vaccines
    • Ensure they don't have heartworms
    • Ensure they are growing well and take if any vitamins

It may seem overwhelming, but trust me it is worth it. Your local pet grooming salon, can take care of most of their hygiene needs, but it will cost you money. Your vet may turn out to be costly, but many times the shelter can provide reduced-cost (maybe free) vaccine that your pup may need to ensure your safety and their health.
